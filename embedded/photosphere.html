Tickets

Gallena UniversityAthleticsTickets

na Ticket Office is open from 1:30-4 p.m. in the main lobby of the Recreation Center and can be reached by telephone at 818-555-9400. All regular season sports games, except basketball and football are free to the public and do not require tickets. Cash and cards are accepted. Football tickets can also be purchased at the football ticket window on the east side of Gallena Stadium. Students receive free admission to all home games with valid student ID. Ticket prices for football and men's basketball are as follows:

General Admission: 

Adult    $8.00
Youth   $6.00 

Reserved Seating:

Adult    $15.00
Youth   $10.00 

Season Tickets:

Football (5 games): $65.00
Men's Basketball (12+ games - $180 value): Adult $125.00; Youth $80.00 per seat
